Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I miss my bus?

If you miss your bus, you will need to wait for the next available bus on the same route. Please note that your ticket is valid only for the specified route and cannot be used on a different route.

Are there any restrictions on using a Daily Pass or Monthly Pass?

Both Daily and Monthly Passes offer you the freedom to travel as much as you want within their respective durations. However, please note that the passes are non-transferable and must be used by the pass holder only.

What documents do I need to book a Daily Pass?

To book a Daily Pass, you will need to provide the last 4 digits of your Aadhaar card. This information is used for verification purposes and helps ensure a secure and streamlined booking process. Rest assured, your privacy and data security are our top priorities, and your information will be handled in accordance with relevant regulations.

Can I cancel a booked ticket?

Currently, we do not offer the option to cancel booked tickets. Once a ticket is confirmed and the payment is processed, it is considered final and non-refundable. We recommend reviewing your travel details carefully before completing your booking. If you have any questions or need assistance, please feel free to reach out to us.

Do I need to print my e-ticket, or can I show it on my phone?

You can show your e-ticket on your phone; there is no need to print it. The conductor will verify the ticket details from your phone to facilitate a hassle-free boarding process. Please make sure that your phone screen is easily readable to ensure a smooth experience.

Can I use my bus ticket for a particular route more than once?

No, each bus ticket for a specific route is valid for a single use only. Once you have used the ticket to travel on the specified route, it cannot be used again. If you need to travel the same route again, you will need to purchase a new ticket for that journey. This policy ensures fair access to all passengers and helps maintain a reliable and efficient travel experience.

What documents are necessary for a Student Monthly Pass?

To obtain a Student Monthly Pass, please prepare the following documents:
Receipt: Provide a copy of your recent fees receipt from your educational institution.
Certificate: Include a bonafide certificate issued by your school or college to confirm your student status.
Proof of Identity (Aadhaar Card): Submit a valid Aadhaar Card for identity verification.
Photo: Attach a passport-sized photo of yourself.
These documents are essential to ensure your eligibility for the Student Monthly Pass. Rest assured, your privacy and data security are important to us, and all documents will be handled in compliance with privacy regulations.
